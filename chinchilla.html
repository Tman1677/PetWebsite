Chinchillas

Do:
  • Have a tall wire cage with multiple levels since chinchillas jump a lot.
  • Provide a bed / nesting box on the bottom floor.
  • Spread gerbil food around tank so gerbils can forage instead of putting it in a bowl.
  • Always keep a supply of quality timothy hay and some small chinchilla pellets.
  • Give them dust baths 1-2 times per 2 weeks.
  • Give them toys to chew on since their teeth grow so much.
Don't:
  • Overheat them- Chinchillas can only survive between 50-75 degrees.
  • Bathe them in water.
  • Keep them in their cage all day- chinchillas need to run in open space (NOT an exercise wheel or hamster ball).
