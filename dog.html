Dogs

Do:
  • Be patient while training your dog.
  • Provide them with plenty of water and healthy food.
  • Allow them time outside to exercise and be active, walk them regularly.
  • Let them out to use the bathroom consistently, clean up their waste in public.
  • Take your dog to the vet regularly and make sure they are up do date on vaccinations.
  • Properly train your dog to obey your commands.
Don't:
  • Allow other people to treat your dog differently than you would.
  • Feed your dog human foods or other pet foods that could make them sick.
  • Overdo it with treats- its unhealthy for your dog.
  • Allow your dog to make unnecessary noise.
  • Leave your dog unattended in a vehicle, especially on hot days.
