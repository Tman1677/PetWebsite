Ferrets

Do:
  • Ideal habitat is a wire cage with a complex lock since ferrest are experts at escaping.
  • Have a carpeted floor, litter box and multiple levels in cage.
  • Food is protein and fat: buy dry ferret food that contains both & they eat 8-10 times a day.
  • Train your ferret to use a litter box.
  • Hold with two hands.
Don't:
  • Neglect it- ferrets are super smart so they need lots of human contact (minimum of 30 min).
  • Keep it in it’s cage all day- ferrets are super curious.
  • Yell at it- ferrets have highly sensitive hearing and won’t trust you if you scare them.
