Fish

Do:
  • Provide them with enough space.
  • Regularly clean the tank and refill the water.
  • Have only compatible breeds of fish in the same habitat.
  • Do a lot of research before putting together your fish tank and buying your fish.
  • Let a newly set up aquarium run for 2 to 3 days before purchasing your first fish.
  • Feed your fish a variety of flake, pellet and frozen foods to make sure they are getting proper nutrition.
Don't:
  • Overcrowd the fish tank.
  • Completely tear down your aquarium to clean it. This will destroy the biological balance with disastrous results.
  • Put the fish back into the water until 24 hours have passed, if you do it before then the fish could die due to minerals in the water.
